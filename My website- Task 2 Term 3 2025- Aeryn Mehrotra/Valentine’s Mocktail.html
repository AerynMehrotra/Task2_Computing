Valentine’s Mocktail❤️

Instructions

Fill a glass with ice cubes.

Pour in cranberry juice & pink lemonade.

Then top with sparkling water.

Stir gently and add honey for extra sweetness.

Garnish with fresh strawberries & mint.

Serve immediately and enjoy!
Ingredients

10 cups Seedless Watermelon Cubes, frozen for at least 24 hours

2-4 tbsp Maple Syrup

Juice of 1 large Lime

1/4 cup Fresh Mint

1 1/2 cup Filtered Water
Utilities

Spoon

Muddler

Glass

Straw
