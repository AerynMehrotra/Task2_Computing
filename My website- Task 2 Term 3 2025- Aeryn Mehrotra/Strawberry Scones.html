Strawberry Scones❤️
Instructions

1.Place the butter in the freezer for 1 hour.

2.Meanwhile, position your oven rack in the upper third of your oven and preheat to 400 degrees F.

3.Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.

4.In a large measuring cup or medium bowl, beat the egg and cream together and set aside.

5.In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

6.Remove the butter from the freezer and grate it directly into the flour.

7.Then, working with your fingers, quickly toss the butter with the flour just until combined.

8.Pour the egg and cream over the flour-butter mixture and with a rubber spatula, stir and fold to combine.

9.Fold in the strawberries and pecans.

10.Divide the dough into two equal-sized balls.

11.Pat them into a 5-inch round, then slice each round into 6 wedges using a lightly floured knife.

12.Transfer scones to the prepared baking sheet.

13.In a small bowl, beat together egg and water to create the egg wash, then gently brush over the tops of the scones.

14.Bake the scones for 18-20 minutes , until they are golden brown on top.

15.Serve immediately.
Ingredients

For the Scones:

5 tablespoons butter frozen as a stick (or chilled and cut into small cubes)

1 large egg very cold

2/3 cup heavy cream very cold

2 cups all-purpose flour

2 tablespoons sugar

1 tablespoon baking powder

1/4 teaspoon salt

1/2 cup strawberries diced

For the Eggwash:

1 large egg

1 tablespoon milk
