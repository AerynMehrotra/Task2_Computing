Cherry Mojito Mocktail❤️

Instructions

1.Place chopped cherries and lime in a glass.

2.Add a little sweetener if using.

3.Muddle together for about 1 minute.

4.If you don't have a muddle, use the back end of a wooden spoon.

5.The idea is to get it small enough to fit though a straw.

6.Clap mint together in your hand to release the scent and add to the glass.

7.Press gently with a muddler a few times.

8.Add ice and top with your choice of sparkling drink.

9.Garnish with lime wheels, mint and/or extra cherries.
Ingredients

5 Cherries roughly chopped

1 Lime quartered

6-8 Mint leaves

1 cup Sparkling water

Sweetener of your choice to taste
Utilities

Spoon

Muddler

Glass

Straw
