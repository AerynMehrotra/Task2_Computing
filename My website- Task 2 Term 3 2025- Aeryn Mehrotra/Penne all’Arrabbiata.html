Penne all’Arrabbiata❤️
Instructions

1.Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.

2.Add garlic, stir for 10 seconds.

3.Add cayenne and chili flakes, cook for 1 minute until garlic is lightly golden.

4.Pour in crushed tomatoes.

5.Season with salt and pepper.
6.Rinse tomato cans with 1/2 cup water and add to the pot.

7.Turn heat to high until it starts bubbling.

8.Reduce to medium and simmer for 15 minutes.

9.Taste at 10 minutes—add sugar if the sauce is too sour.

10.Boil water in a separate pot with 1 tbsp salt.

11.Add penne and cook per packet instructions until al dente.

12.Before draining, stir pasta and scoop out 1 cup of cooking water.

13.Drain pasta.

14.Add drained pasta to the sauce.

15.Pour in 1/2 cup pasta water.

16.Toss well over heat until pasta is coated.

17.Add more water if needed to loosen.

18.Divide into bowls.

19.Top with Parmesan and parsley if using.

20.Serve immediately.
