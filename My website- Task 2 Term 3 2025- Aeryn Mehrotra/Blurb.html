Blurb

The majority of online cookbook websites lack individuality and mostly appeal to an older audience—mainly adults.

They’re all very similar in design and feel, and the recipes they contain are often unhealthy, overly complicated, and take too much time to make.

There’s a dire need for a website that focuses on recipes that are healthy, easy to make, and accessible for everyone—no matter their age, cooking experience, or how much time they have.

It would make cooking more enjoyable again, turning it back into what it used to be: something people did for quality time, as a hobby, or as a form of self-expression.

A site like that would stand out by being more inclusive, modern, and catering to a wider range of people.
