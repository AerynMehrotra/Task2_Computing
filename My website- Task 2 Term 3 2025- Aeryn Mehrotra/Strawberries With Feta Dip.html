Strawberries With Feta Dip❤️

Instructions

1.Preheat the oven to 350 F and line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.

2.Wash strawberries and remove stems.

3.Slice each berry in half or quarters (depending on size).

4.Spread berries on a lined baking sheet.

5.Whisk balsamic vinegar and honey together.

6.Drizzle over strawberries and toss until combined.

7.Spread in an even layer on a baking sheet, then transfer to the oven and roast for about 30-35 minutes.

8.They'll start to soften and release their juices when done.

9.Meanwhile, make whipped feta by adding the feta cheese, greek yogurt, olive oil, and pepper to a food processor.

10.Blend ingredients together for a few minutes until smooth, scraping down with a spatula as needed.

11.Transfer to the fridge and chill for 30-60 minutes.

12.When the strawberries are done roasting, remove them from the oven and let them cool for 5-10 minutes.

13.Transfer them to a container and chill in the fridge .

14.When you're ready to serve ,transfer the whipped feta to a large bowl and top with roasted strawberries.

15.Serve with toasted crusty bread for dipping.

16.Garnish with freshly chopped basil, an extra drizzle of olive oil, and a sprinkle of black pepper.
Ingredients

Roasted Strawberries:

2 lbs fresh strawberries

2 Tbsp balsamic vinegar

2 tsp honey

Whipped Feta:

200 grams feta cheese

1/2 cup plain greek yogurt

2 Tbsp olive oil

Black pepper, to taste

Serve With:

Crusty bread

Fresh basil, chopped
