Watermelon Slushie❤️

Instructions

1.First, let the Frozen Watermelon chunks sit at room temperature for 5-10 minutes to defrost some.

2.Then, add the Watermelon, 2 tbsp Maple Syrup, Lime Juice, Mint, and Water to a high-speed blender.

3.Pulse the blender until the Watermelon starts to break up some, then blend to form a thick, slushie consistency.

4.Adjust the Maple Syrup to taste, adding more if necessary.

5.If the mixture is too thick for your liking, you can also add in extra water.

6.Divide equally into 4 glasses, serve, and enjoy!
Ingredients> 10 cups Seedless Watermelon Cubes, frozen for at least 24 hours

2-4 tbsp Maple Syrup

Juice of 1 large Lime

1/4 cup Fresh Mint

1 1/2 cup Filtered Water
Utilities

Spoon

Muddler

Glass

Straw
